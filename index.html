#2020-2-24 Notes

I am looking forward to being a P.H.D student on CV/AI field. However, I do not have anything to show about not only my capacity, but also research results on my cv or any titles which make others feel respect for me. Hence, I hope my blog can be a fair place to show you what I have done, what I can do, how big is my determination. But it is not the aim for this note, the real motivation for this note is that I drunk beer, pass the reply, fail one exam and send my favorite girl home.

I am learning contrastive learning. I found that knowledge I undestood now is outdated, or published before 2018. But I also found that I can understand even the best papers in the best conference, or the milestone work in this field. To be honest, they are not very hard. I start to imagine that I also can do some gorgeous work, publish a paper in CVPR, invent a popular model(I have already named my model: Couple-NET).

However, I am afraid of my this kind of state. I never succeeded, always fail in any exam, always disappointed who notice me. I do not know how hard I should try to achieve this kind of success. I am not a guy who only talk. I convince that I am real. I have some real thing. I am not a fake? Really? Maybe no. I dont know.

To sum up, good luck Xiaoqi. Hope this year is your harvest year.
